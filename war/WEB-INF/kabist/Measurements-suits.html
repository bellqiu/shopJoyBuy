Measurements

In order to make the best garments possible for you, HoneyBuy uses 16 body measurements to create a completely custom suit that's meant to fit you perfectly. We guide you through our online measurement process step-by-step with easy to follow instructions. It only takes 10 minutes and can be done from the comfort of your own home. You don't need a tailor, only a good friend.

You'll be prompted to get measured once you're ready to checkout - after choosing from a full selection of suits, shirts and outerwear and customizing the details to match your style.

Once your order is placed, one of our Master Tailors will take your body measurements and create a custom pattern just for you. This is a one time process, but you can always refine your garment measurements later on.
To refine your measurement, please login to My Profile then click My Measurements to edit.

What you will need:

  • A Fabric Tape Measure. (Buy from local store quickly, we will refund the cost up to $9.99 when the order is completed or Leave Message to us then we will mail you one for free.)
  • Ten to twenty minutes.

It would help if:

  • You had assistance from a friend when measuring (although the majority of the measurements can easily be self-taken, we have found the process to be faster if you have someone readily available to assist you. In addition, if you would rather use a local tailor or seamstress, we will refund the measuring cost up to $29.99 in the form of an in store coupon).
  • You wore a good fitting dress-shirt and slacks. If you do not have these items, any form-fitting clothing will suffice (to include a t-shirt and boxers). No jeans and empty your pockets, please!
  • You do not have time now, why not download Measurement Guide, print out, and submit later?

About Measuring:

  • When taking measurements the tape should be held flat against the body, it should be held snug enough so not droop, but never so tight as to feel restrictive.
  • All measurements should be taken to the nearest quarter (.25) inch.
  • Do not add wiggle room to your measurements. We will adjust the fit based off of your selected preferences and build.
